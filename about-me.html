About Me

My name is Elle Huang. I was born in Taishan, China, a small village in Guangzhou province, and grew up in New York City. Through my parents, I developed an appreciation for language and culture. I speak fluently three dialects of Chinese and I am proficient in German.

As an undergraduate business student majoring in Marketing and German Language at the University of Notre Dame, I have a passion for social entrepreneurship and international development. I am interested in sustainable development in developing regions around the world, specifically urban slums. As a first generation college student and immigrant to the United States, I am especially interested in examining the role of social mobility and education in domestic and international development.
